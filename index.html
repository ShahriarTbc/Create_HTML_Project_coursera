Urban Gardening: Growing Green in the City

Introduction to Urban Gardening

Urban gardening is the practice of cultivating, processing, and distributing food in or around urban areas. It connects city dwellers with nature and encourages sustainable living.

Why You Should Start an Urban Garden

Urban gardening offers numerous benefits that improve quality of life:

  • Enhances mental health and reduces stress.
  • Provides access to fresh, organic produce.
  • Builds community connections and social interaction.
